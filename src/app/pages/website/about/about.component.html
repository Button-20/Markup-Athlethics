About Us.

Markup Athletics is a non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering African high school student athletes with the tools they need to succeed both academically and athletically. Our platform serves as a bridge between talented student athletes in Africa and college coaches in the US. We believe that excellence in sports and education can go hand in hand, and we are passionate about creating opportunities for young talents to study and compete at renowned institutions in the United States.

At Markup Athletics, we have a team of dedicated professionals who verify each student athlete on the platform, ensuring that coaches have access to the most talented and well-prepared candidates. Our analytical tools help showcase the skills and potential of our students, making it easier for coaches to identify the perfect fit for their teams.

We are more than just a platform; we are a family for our student athletes. We partner with an educational consulting firm to provide guidance and support for exam preparation and visa processing. Once our students are accepted into US colleges, we continue to monitor their progress, connecting them with mentors and established athletes to help them thrive.

Our ultimate goal is to create lasting opportunities for our student athletes, not only during their college years but also beyond. We strive to assist them in securing professional sports contracts and becoming leaders in their respective fields. With the motto "Bringing the Advantage," we are committed to transforming the lives of African student athletes and leaving a positive impact on the world of sports and education.

01.

Our Mission.

Empowering African high school student athletes to achieve academic and sports excellence by providing a platform that connects them with college coaches in the US. We aim to bridge the gap, promote education and sports abroad, and create opportunities for talented young athletes in Africa to study and compete at a high level in the United States.

02.

Our Vision.

To be the leading platform that nurtures and elevates the dreams of African high school student athletes, fostering a new generation of empowered individuals who excel academically and athletically, while embracing cultural diversity and becoming global ambassadors of sportsmanship and education.
