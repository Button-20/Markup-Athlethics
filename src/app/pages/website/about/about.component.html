About Us.

Mark-up athletics is a non-governmental organization (NGO) that is dedicated to facilitating opportunities for African high school sports talent to connect with coaches in America. We act as a bridge between talented young athletes and coaches who can help you further develop your skills and potentially pursue academic and athletic opportunities in the United States. Our organization focuses on scouting, identifying, and nurturing promising sports talent in African high schools, and then facilitating the process of connecting them with American coaches and educational institutions. By doing so, we aim to create a pathway for athletes to access better training, education, and career prospects in the world of sports, while also fostering cultural exchange and global sports development.

01.

Our Mission.

To empower and uplift African high school sports talent by creating opportunities for them to connect with coaches and educational institutions in America. We are dedicated to identifying, nurturing, and facilitating the development of these athletes, enabling them to pursue both their athletic and academic dreams while fostering cross-cultural exchanges.

02.

Our Vision.

Our vision is to be a catalyst for positive change in the lives of African high school sports talents, providing them with a pathway to success on and off the field. We aspire to become the premier organization in connecting these young athletes with American coaches, unlocking their full potential and creating a global community of well-rounded, educated, and accomplished individuals who contribute to the world of sports and beyond.
