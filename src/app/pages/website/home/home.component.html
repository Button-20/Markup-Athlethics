MARKUP ATHLETICS

Who we are...

Markup Athletics is a non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering African high school student athletes with the tools they need to succeed both academically and athletically. Our platform serves as a bridge between talented student athletes in Africa and college coaches in the US... Read more.

Here’s how it works for students.

Empowering African high school student athletes to achieve academic and sports excellence by providing a platform that connects them with college coaches in the US

1
Signup and profile creation.

Sign up by creating a personalized profile with essential details.

2
Verification and Analytics.

After verification, a comprehensive analytics of your performance is generated.

3
EMPOWERMENT and Visibility.

Once verified and analyzed, your profile is published and ready for view by college coaches.

4
preparation andsupport.

Get assistance with aptitude tests, English proficiency test and VISA preparation for your transition.

5
Mentorship and Growth.

Connect with established sports personalities who serve as mentors, providing guidance and support.

We aim to bridge the gap, promote education and sports abroad, and create opportunities for talented young athletes in Africa to study and compete at a high level in the United States.
